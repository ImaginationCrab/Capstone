FAQ

Questions & Answers

What is HTSync and how does it work? +
HTSync is an AI-powered import compliance tool. You enter your product details in plain language, and our system searches a live, continuously updated HTS database to return the most accurate tariff code, duty estimate, and an AI confidence score — all in under a minute.
Do I need to understand HTS codes to use HTSync? +
No. HTSync is built specifically for professionals who aren't customs experts. Just describe your product in plain language — the system handles all the classification logic.
How accurate is the HTS code identification? +
Our classification engine is tested against known product-HTS pairings and verified duty amounts. Every result includes a confidence rating so you know exactly how reliable the match is and when human review is advisable.
What does the AI confidence score mean? +
Every classification receives a High, Medium, or Low confidence rating with a plain-language explanation. A Low score triggers a clear warning to double-check before proceeding — reducing the risk of costly misclassifications.
How does HTSync handle tariff changes and updates? +
Our database syncs continuously with official government tariff schedule sources, ensuring recommendations always reflect the most current codes and rates — never outdated data that could lead to penalties.
